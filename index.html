Whats Our Motive?

We think they everybody deserves a pair of shoes.

In Africa, over 20,000,000 children are without shoes on their feet.

Africa

Africa is known for its lack of recorces such as food and water.

With struggles like these, people shouldnt need to worry about having shoes and clothes. They should be focased on getting food and water.

Imagine urself living a life without shoes. Why would you ever want to live like that. These kids play sports and participate in many other activities. Not having shoes is a huge roadblock in thir lives.

The Children

These children are not provided with much in their lives.

So many of these kids need to fight for food, water, and clothes. Donating shoes could make their lives so much better.
